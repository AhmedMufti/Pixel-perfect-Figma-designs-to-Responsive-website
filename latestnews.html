Today Taleem Shareef Played in 381 After Zuhar Prayer

16 Jul 2025 04:50 PM
As-salaamu 'alaykum wa rahmatullaahi wa barakaatuhu

16 July 2025 Budh
20 Muharram-ul-Haraam 1447 Hijri

Multan Shareef 381-A mein Zohar ki namaz ke baad chalne wala track

Video Taleem Shareef: VM-0337
25 April 2008 Jummah Mehfli

Na'at Shareef
• Hain dil jigar yeh pyaase mere
Ata karen aap nazar ka daaru

• Allah ki rehmat us simt lapke
Jis simt jaye naam-e-Muhammad ﷺ

Taleem Shareef
Aaqa ji ﷺ ki nazar mubarak, bhaiyon ki islaah, Sheikh ji mein Aaqa ji ﷺ ka aks mubarak,
Rahmat-ul-lil-'aalameen kaafiron ke liye bhi rehmat, kasrat-e-darood, silsil e ka darood (Sallallahu 'alayhi wa sallam)
aur Darood-e-Ibrahimi ka pehla hissa, Sheikh ji pe waswasa faiz mein rukawat ka sabab
